Why It Matters

Big Horn is a close-knit Wyoming community that values youth, health, and connection. However, the community faces a growing challenge:

  • No local indoor recreation facility, impacting youth through seniors.
  • Limited space for Early Childhood programs, which continue to grow.
  • Harsh climate conditions limit utilization of many outdoor recreation facilities.
  • Lack of a central hub for connection and engagement - outside of our schools.

The Vision

This is more than just a building – it’s a catalyst for health, education, and unity. By combining recreation and education, we will:

  • Enhance quality of life for all ages.
  • Support families with state-of-the-art early childhood education spaces.
  • Give youth a place to be active, build confidence, and develop life skills.
  • Leave a lasting legacy for generations to come.

Recreation Center Features

  • Available to our Community
  • Hardwood Sports Courts
  • Walking Track
  • Indoor Turf Area
  • Fitness & Weight Room
  • Early Childhood Wing
  • Multi-Use Community Spaces
